rainbow agenda features

  • Manage Meetings

    Book your recurring meetings in advance and forget about sending reminders or setting up a new meeting every week.

  • Manage Attendees

    Quickly add or remove attendees with minimal effort.

  • Manage Agendas

    Prepare agenda items in a simple and visually pleasing way. Copyprevious agenda topics that have not been discussed or need further clarification.

  • Send Invitations

    Set automatic invitations. Minus one responsibility!
